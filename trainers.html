Maroubra Outdoor Bootcamp

The Maroubra outdoor fitness group is a committed group of locals - there is no image or ego, just people who want to train as a group and have some fun at the same time. It is not like your average bootcamp in maroubra, we treat you as an individual, and all work together to achieve our fitness and wellbeing goals. One client even arrives at sessions with a mug of tea in hand! Apparently we should all be wary of the day she arrives at session with out it!
