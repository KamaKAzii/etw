Raw Cheese Cake

By: Dan D'ambrosio

10 Comments

Tags: Nutrition, beach, fun, group, outdoors, outdoor, palm, beach

My friends think that eating healthy is boring! It is not. Here is a SUGAR FREE, DAIRY FREE, GLUTEN FREE but DELICIOUS recipe for all those sweet heads... oh and did I mention it's GUILT FREE (just don't eat the whole thing).

This went down very well after training for Ash's B'day. Simple, healthy and interesting.

Crust

  • 1 1/2 cups macadamia nuts
  • 1/2 cup dates
  • 1 pinch sea salt

Cheescake filling

  • 3 cups cashews
  • 3/4 cup lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup agave or honey
  • 3/4 cup coconut oil

Fruit topping ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups berries (blueberries, strawberries, or rasberries)
  • 1/2 cup dates
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil

Method

  • Soak the macadamia nuts and cashew nuts in a bowl of water (separately) overnight or for 8 hours. Drain before use.
  • Throw the macadamia nuts, salt and dates into your food processor. Mix until well processed, will look like cookie dough.
  • Line 8x8 baking tin with baking paper. This will help get the pieces out. Press the macadamia nuts and date mixture down into the pan (or you can use muffin cases) to form the crust.
  • Throw all 'cream-cheese' filling ingredients into your high-speed blender and blend until smooth. Might take a few minutes. Don't leave the blender on continuously or you'll heat the mixture. Might need to add a little water to facilitate blending. (Try to add as little water as possible.)
  • Pour mixture on top of crust.
  • Place into freezer for 30 mins or so to firm the crust up.
  • Throw your berries, dates and coconut oil into a blender. Blend until nice and smooth. Spoon mixture on top of the crust/creamcheese. Place the raw cheesecake back in freezer. Freeze for about 5 hours or so.
  • Defrost before eating, or just slice from freezer and enjoy.
